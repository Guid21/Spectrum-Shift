Game Guide & FAQ
Main Game Goal
Combine tiles of the same color to create new colors and score points. The goal is to reach the highest color levels and achieve the maximum score!
Color System (12 Levels)
Color Levels:
1. Coral Red (2 points)
2. Sunset Orange (4 points)
3. Golden Yellow (8 points)
4. Lime Green (16 points)
5. Emerald (32 points)
6. Turquoise (64 points)
7. Sky Blue (128 points)
8. Royal Blue (256 points)
9. Deep Purple (512 points)
10. Violet (1024 points)
11. Rose Pink (2048 points)
12. Sunset Gold (4096 points)
Currency System (Gems)
Gems are earned when merging tiles. The amount depends on the tile level. Use gems in the shop to purchase upgrades. Important: rewards decrease as score increases to create difficulty.
Rainbow Tiles
Rare tiles with an iridescent background. Can merge with any regular tile. Base spawn chance is 1% (can be increased in the shop). When merging two rainbow tiles, a rainbow tile of the highest level is created.
"Blast" Ability
Unlock: When reaching the 6th color level.
How it works: Destroys groups of adjacent tiles of the same color.
Cost: 8 + increases with score.
Cooldown: 8 seconds + increases with score.
"Undo" Ability
Not available by default - must be purchased in the shop. Allows you to undo the last move. Number of uses is limited and resets when starting a new game.
Blockers (Obstacles)
Appear after 100 points with a 15% probability. Gray tiles with a red X that cannot be merged. Create additional challenge. Can only be destroyed with the Blast ability.
Shop and Upgrades
Extra Undo: +1 undo (25 + increases with purchases)
Rainbow Boost: +3% rainbow tile chance (40 + increases)
Grid Upgrade: Upgrade grid from 4x4 to 5x5 (100, one-time)
Controls
Desktop: Arrow keys
Mobile: Swipe on screen
Blast mode: Click on tile to blast
All tiles move in the selected direction until they stop.
Scoring System and Difficulty
Points: Depend on tile level (2-4096 points)
Difficulty: As score increases, ability costs rise, rewards decrease, blockers and higher-level tiles appear more often.
Best Score: Saved between sessions.
Difficulty Features
High Difficulty:
No undos by default
Low rainbow tile chance (1%)
Blockers appear after 100 points
Rising ability costs
Decreasing merge rewards
Sometimes level 2-3 tiles spawn immediately
